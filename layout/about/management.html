Puneet Setia

Apparently, I have chosen various career options from an engineer to a lecturer, from a certified associate in project management to an education services manager. No matter which sector you are working in; education is always indispensable. Education is not restricted to learning only; but it also leads to continuous improvement, resulting in adroitness in changing environment.

Talking about myself, when I came to Canada as an international student I suffer many hardship such as cultural shock, homesickness and too much confusion about how to make new friends. I also struggled with the different and modern model of education followed by Canadian students which emphasize on the continuous involvement of students for their successful completion of credentials. My student life could be less challenging if appropriate information was provided to me beforehand. I believe that my suffering could be learning to many folks. Now, I got an opportunity where I could support and help many students like me. They can leverage the services provided by our company in a very fruitful way. I encourage folks to take a free advice even if they wish to apply on their own or through any other educational agents so that we can still make a significant impact in the community.
